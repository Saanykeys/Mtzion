About Us

Mt zion history dates back to 1841. Over the years, we have been blessed with faithful leaders and members who have worked tirelessly to serve our community and spread the gospel. Whether you are new to the faith, looking for a church home, or just curious about Christianity, we invite you to join us for worship and see what our church is all about. Our church is committed to sharing the love and message of Jesus Christ with the world, and we would love for you to join us on this journey. Today, we continue to build on a foundation built by Richard Allen, as we seek to honor God and serve others.Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope to see you soon!
